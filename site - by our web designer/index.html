What is Mafia?

The Game „Mafia” is for everyone, that want to who want to have fun and helps for socializing.The game has 5 roles – destiny killers, doctor, police and civilians.Destiny knows the history and says it to the players. Murderers take the life of one of the players in the morning, the doctor can save a player policeman checks if one of the participants is a murderer, and civilians have no role at night. During the day all wake up and vote to kill someone. The action repeat while one party does not win.
